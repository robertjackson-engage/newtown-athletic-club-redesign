Five reasons to try Six Zone this week

Ask around the club and you'll hear it constantly: "I'm a total Six Zone junkie." Here's why our signature 35-minute, heart-rate-driven workout has such a devoted following.

1. It fits any schedule. A new class starts every five minutes — just show up. 2. It meets you where you are. Your Myzone belt tracks your effort, so you compete with yourself. 3. It never gets stale. Six stations, rotating focus: upper, lower and full body through the week. 4. The energy is unreal. Great coaches, great music, a room full of people pushing together. 5. It works. Thirty-five focused minutes and you're done — sweaty, strong and smiling.

Want to try it? Grab a free pass and come find out why it's so hard to quit.
